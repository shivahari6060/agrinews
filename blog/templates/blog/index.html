Welcome to agriculture blog site

Agri-News Blog

This is the agri-news site, which is created to disseminate information acquired from research to the layman farmers. NARC, main agricultural research wing of Nepal annually conducts several research to develop further prolific and productive good agronomic practices. Also, NARC has authority to conduct varietal trials and release new varieties of agronomic crops. Similarly, Institution which conduct undergraduate and graduate curriculum also conducts research which in result get printed but the acquired knowledge doesn't hit the targeted farmers. Thus this site is created to bridge the gap on information between all the farmers, researchers, and extension workers.

Posts

Posts section on the header of site takes you to the blog section of the site, where several posts are listed based on the release date. The categories section include the category of posts in the post list. The search input at the top bar helps you to find the desired post for you. Please go through this section and provide valuable suggestions.

About/Contact Us

This site is created on the initiation of the agriculture graduates, who feels the gap between research and extension and wish to created site with proper agriculture blog articles which give precise idea for the farmers and extension workers. There are already thousands of site available on agriculture information but there are very few sites that are specific on the genotype and good agronomic practices suitable for Nepalese farmer's condition.
